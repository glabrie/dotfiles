* The emulators debate

Terminal emulators can be a contentious subject. It's not the Emacs vs Vim war of old but it still elicit some attachment to their favorite one. Honestly, I don't have a strong opinion on emulators. I've used many at various times in my life and never ran across something that would make me really consider switching. Last time I switched was because I liked [[https://sw.kovidgoyal.net/kitty/][Kitty]]'s' image support and cursor trail.

That's it. There's things out there like [[https://ghostty.org/][Ghostty]] that are really cool project but as long as kitty doesn't give me any reasons to switch, I'll still be using it in 10 years.
